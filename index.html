Our story

In 2020, our team of four business school students passionated in sneakers and retail of all kind of stuff had a great idea : create their own retail company of sneakers. Started from scratch in a tiny flat of Bordeaux, we are now a 50-employee company full of big ambitions (and we have our own locals now). Laurene is the CEO of Sneak'in, she's the one who came up with the idea for this incredible adventure. Jean-Gabin is our Treasurer, a graduate in finance from Kedge BS. Mallory is in charge of our communication and Bryan of marketing, they are essential to promote the brand on social networks.
